What is AI

Artificial Intelligence (AI) refers to the simulation of human intelligence in machines that are programmed to think, learn, and make decisions. AI encompasses various subfields, including machine learning, natural language processing, robotics, and computer vision, which enable machines to perform tasks that typically require human intelligence.
